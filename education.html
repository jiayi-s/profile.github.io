Research Experience

    May 2019 - December 2019, Columbia University
    • Performed preliminary analysis of large-scale Electronic Health Records (EHR) data at family level.

    • Illustrated differential gene expression by generating a variety of publication quality figures using R/Bioconductor packages.
    • Developed a workflow using R to process RNA-sequencing data to visualize differential gene expression.
  • Identifying Distant Human-Yeast Orthologue Genes on PomBase
    09/2017-03/2018, University College London
